The Journey

Student · Learner · Dreamer

At 18 years old, I made the choice to pursue sustainable mental health, build positive connections, continue education, and to gain positive work experiences. I hit the ground running by moving from Arizona to California, staying with friends and their families while working gig jobs to save money. I walked dogs, delivered food on bike, and worked in a sandwich shop. Soon, I was presented with the opportunity to work for the Bay Area Rapid Transit District (BART) as a user experience survey technician. I took that opportunity seriously and was promoted from contractor to permanent employment, while still managing school as best I could.

While continuing this journey, I started leasing an apartment, continued progress with classes, started and funded my LLC, built a growing network of artists, creatives, and entrepreneurs across the Bay Area, got a dog, performed original songs for hundreds of people, navigated relationships, transitioned out of BART into full-time school, provided professional business consultations and services, traveled internationally, built strong mental and physical health, and continue learning whenever I get the chance.

I would never boast that I did this alone. My family and friends helped at every step with jobs, opportunities, places to stay, food to eat, and even served this country in combat to hold up my dreams. I am deeply thankful to you all.

I believe God, the Creator, is the Keeper of my future. No matter what I may plan or achieve, everything I do is written according to the Will of Allah. Through humanity and iniquity, I only try to act as a reflection of Grace and Divinity.

Now, I have graduated from Berkeley City College with:

  • Associate of Arts for Transfer – Business Administration
  • Associate of Arts for Transfer – Economics
  • Associate of Arts – Liberal Arts (Emphasis on Arts and Humanities)
What's Next

Onward & Upward

Depending on the program and institution I decide on, I will be continuing in 2026 or 2027. Some educational directions I am pursuing are: California Institute of the Arts and other institutions with creative entrepreneurial programs, the California State University system, the University of California system, and studying abroad in regions like West Africa, South East Asia, the Caribbean, or Europe.
